# Foundations of Social and Web Analytics
- Science of collecting, measuring, and interpreting digital data from the social media, or websites to enable smarter decision making
- social and web analytics is used in all industries as public data is everywhere. It needs to be collected and analysed 

## Types of Analytics
1. Descriptive = perform data analysis based on the description given to understand what happened
2. Diagnostic = perform further data analysis to understand why did the issue happened?
3. Predictive = predict the result (what will happen) based on the data analysis performed
4. Prescriptive = prescribe solution based on the data analysis performed.
