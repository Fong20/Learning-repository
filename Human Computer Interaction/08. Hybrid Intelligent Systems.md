- Back-propagationlearningalgorithmcannotguaranteeanoptimalsolution.
- The optimaltopologyfortheneuralnetworkisdifficulttoidentify,oftenchosen by meansofheuristics(trialanderror+experience).
- An evolutionaryneuralnetworkuses genetic algorithm to findtheoptimalweights and topologyofa neuralnetwork.
▶ Multiple neuralnetworkswithdifferentweightsandtopologyaregenerated.
▶ The parameters(weightsandtopology)ofeachneuralnetworkareencodedasa
chromosomefortheGA.
▶ GA optimisesthechromosomesbymeansofcrossoverandmutation.
▶ As aresultwegetan‘evolved’neuralnetwork.
